Welcome to IMeditate IIT Delhi!

We are in the process of building our new website. In the meantime, here's a bit about us:

IMeditate IIT Delhi is a vibrant community of over 600 meditation enthusiasts, with 50 active members, including students, faculty, staff of IIT Delhi, and residents from nearby regions. Led by four dedicated faculty members, who are highly skilled meditation teachers, we engage in various activities.

Our activities range from weekly meditation sessions, music meditations, to enriching outings and advanced meditation programs in serene locations like the Himalayas, Nilgiri Mountains, Bangalore, Rishikesh, Haridwar, Dharamshala, and Darjeeling.

Join us as we create a space of peace and mindfulness within IIT Delhi and beyond. We appreciate your patience and look forward to unveiling our new website soon!
